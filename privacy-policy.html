Privacy Policy

EuAI — Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how EuAI handles information in connection with its educational content creation and publishing service. EuAI is operated by Lorenzo Ferro as a personal project focused on AI education for the nonprofit sector.

2. Information We Collect

EuAI is primarily a content creation and publishing tool. We do not directly collect personal information from viewers of our TikTok content. When interacting with our content on TikTok, any data collection is governed by TikTok's own privacy policy.

3. TikTok API Usage

EuAI uses TikTok's Content Posting API to publish video content. This integration requires authentication with TikTok and operates within TikTok's API terms and data handling guidelines. We access only the permissions necessary to publish content (video upload, posting).

4. Data We Do Not Collect

EuAI does not collect, store, or process personal data of TikTok users, viewers, or followers. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or any third-party tracking tools beyond what TikTok provides natively.

5. AI-Generated Content

Content published by EuAI is generated using artificial intelligence tools. No personal data is used in the content generation process. All content is educational in nature and focused on AI topics relevant to the nonprofit sector.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any authentication credentials and API tokens used to operate the service. Access to the publishing system is restricted to the service operator.

7. Third-Party Services

EuAI interacts with TikTok's platform via their official API. Users should review TikTok's privacy policy for information about how TikTok handles data. We may also use AI service providers (such as OpenRouter) for content generation, subject to their respective privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

If you believe any content published by EuAI affects your privacy, please contact us through our TikTok profile. As we operate under EU jurisdiction, we respect all rights granted under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please reach out via the EuAI TikTok profile.
